These are your combat stats, they'll what you'll need to keep track of if you want to survive:

  • Armor Class: A measure of how difficult it is to hit your character in combat.
  • Initiative: A measure of how quickly your character reacts in combat.
  • Speed: A measure of how far your character can move in a turn.
  • Proficiency Bonus: A measure of your character's skill in certain areas.
  • Hit Points: A measure of how much damage your character can take before falling unconscious.
  • Hit Dice: A measure of your character's ability to recover hit points.

These are your core stats, used in nearly every single part of the game for nearly every single situation

  • Strength: A measure of your character's physical power.
  • Dexterity: A measure of your character's agility and reflexes.
  • Constitution: A measure of your character's health and stamina.
  • Intelligence: A measure of your character's mental acuity and reasoning.
  • Wisdom: A measure of your character's perception and insight.
  • Charisma: A measure of your character's force of personality and charm.
